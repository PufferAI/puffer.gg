Puffer Tactics

A turn-based tactical combat game where players face off in an isometric arena. Cast spells, control space, and outmaneuver your opponent in this fast-paced tactical battle.

Controls:

  • Press Q or Backspace to exit
  • Press 1-5 to cast spells
  • Press Space to skip turn
  • Use mouse to select targets
